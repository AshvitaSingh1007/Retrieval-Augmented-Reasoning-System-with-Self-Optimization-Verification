TITLE:
AI Evaluation Framework

DEFINITION:
A system to measure AI output quality.

KEY COMPONENTS:
- Accuracy
- Relevance
- Faithfulness

WORKFLOW:
1. Generate answer
2. Compare with ground truth
3. Score performance

FAILURE MODES:
- Weak benchmarks
- Biased datasets

IMPROVEMENTS:
- Better datasets
- Multi-metric evaluation

APPLICATIONS:
- Model testing
- Benchmarking

RESEARCH INSIGHTS:
- Evaluation is as important as generation
