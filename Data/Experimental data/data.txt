Fusion energy is a process where two light atomic nuclei combine to form a heavier nucleus, releasing a large amount of energy.
This process powers the Sun and stars.
Fusion energy is considered a clean and sustainable energy source because it produces no direct carbon emissions.
The European Union invests heavily in renewable energy such as solar, wind, and fusion research.
Renewable energy includes sources like solar power, wind power, and hydropower.
Energy policies in Europe focus on sustainability, energy independence, and reducing carbon emissions.
