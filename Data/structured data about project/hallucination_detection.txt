TITLE:
Hallucination Detection

DEFINITION:
Identifying when AI generates unsupported or false information.

KEY COMPONENTS:
- Evidence mapping
- Confidence scoring

WORKFLOW:
1. Generate answer
2. Compare with context
3. Flag unsupported parts

FAILURE MODES:
- Overconfidence
- Missing evidence

IMPROVEMENTS:
- Verification layers

APPLICATIONS:
- Trustworthy AI systems

RESEARCH INSIGHTS:
- Hallucination is a core challenge in LLMs
